Editorial Art Direction
Direction & creative management of external talent for a monthly print & digital publication.

My role as Art Director for the publication shown below was centered on creating content to help evolve the modern and minimal visual style that this magazine was well known for. To achieve this, the position required end-to-end talent and client management: interviewing, commissioning, and directing a diverse network of freelance illustrators, photographers, and copywriters to translate complex editorial themes and client briefs into the final product. 

I oversaw the visual narrative of each issue, ensuring the pacing and expressive creative execution of the layouts, photography, and illustration remained intentionally familiar, while continuously pushing the magazine's visual style into fresh, forward-looking territory. This process relied on leading small teams through intense production cycles, utilizing a fast-paced workflow similar to digital 'agile' methodologies, to deliver beautiful work on tight publishing deadlines.
