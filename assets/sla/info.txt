SugaringLA
Comprehensive brand evolution and digital strategy to scale a skincare franchise nationally.

When the founder of Los Angeles-based skincare brand sugaringLA looked to scale beyond their Southern California footprint, our agency was brought on to lead their nationwide brand evolution. The objective was to reposition the business from a regional service provider, into a scalable national brand spanning physical franchise locations and at-home CPG product line.

Acting as the digital strategist and creative lead, I helped to manage this comprehensive brand transformation. The repositioning began by conducting competitive market analysis and target audience profiling, allowing us to refine the brand's identity for national growth.

We translated this new strategic positioning into a foundational logomark that references the organic nature of their signature product, and across an entire ecosystem — designing a minimalistic and modern e-commerce web experience, franchise marketing collateral, product packaging, and establishing social media guidelines. This work successfully drove increases in both brick-and-mortar franchise traffic and online DTC e-commerce sales.
